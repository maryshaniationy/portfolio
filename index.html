About Me

Engineer. Educator. Builder.

I'm a Kenyan Electrical & Electronics Engineer specialising in Instrumentation, Control, and Renewable Energy. I hold a BSc in Instrumentation & Control Engineering from Egerton University and have worked across some of East Africa's most technically demanding environments.

My current roles span an Instrumentation & Control PSIP internship at JKUAT, and an Equipment Technician role at Futop Solar EPZ Kenya — one of the continent's rare semiconductor-grade PV cell fabrication plants. I also run Zindua Labs, a weekend STEM enrichment programme for Kenyan students.

I'm driven by the belief that engineering education and clean energy infrastructure can change outcomes at scale — and I'm actively building both.
